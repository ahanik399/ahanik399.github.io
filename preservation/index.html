Preservation Analysis

Applied operational intelligence derived from documented failure patterns observed in property preservation investigations.

What We Observed

  • Photo-related work order failures occur before QC review
  • Documentation gaps drive rework, delays, and deductions
  • Loss accumulates silently at the submission stage
  • QC processes often react too late to prevent damage

What We Detect

  • Pre-submission photo risk signals
  • Documentation readiness gaps
  • Vendor-induced loss patterns
  • Repeated failure indicators across work orders

How It Works

  • No workflow disruption
  • No submission blocking
  • No platform interference
  • Operators remain in control
