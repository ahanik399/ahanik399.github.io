Property Preservation Operational Analysis

Investigative analysis of property preservation workflows focused on profit leakage, cash flow risk, and documentation failure.

Property preservation businesses rarely struggle because work disappears. They struggle when value quietly erodes between the field, the office, and the balance sheet.

This analysis domain examines how preservation activity behaves once operational data is connected end-to-end — revealing where margins are retained, where they stall, and where they are permanently lost.
