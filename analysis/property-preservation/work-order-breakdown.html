The Grass Recut Paradox

Grass Recut is the single highest-volume work type with 138 orders — but generates an income ratio of just 0.71, meaning the operation loses $0.29 for every dollar received. Total loss from this category alone is $1,839 at a 16.2% leakage rate. It dominates volume while destroying value. Any operational scale in this category accelerates losses proportionally.

Bid Approval Is the Engine

Bid Approval at 38 orders carries a 1.83 income ratio and is the only high-volume type with a clear scale signal. Despite a 21.1% leakage rate, the absolute loss per order is low because margins are wider. This is the category that supports the entire operation. Protecting its margin while controlling the $20,163 leakage concentration within it is the single highest-value intervention available.
