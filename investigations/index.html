Blood Routes

An open-source investigative dataset on bus-related fatalities in Dhaka, the structural incentives of the waybill system, and the accountability failures that perpetuate them.

Published February 2026 • 12 min read • Road Safety

Primary Findings

  • The Data Gap: Official figures undercount fatalities by a factor of 5.8× compared to WHO estimates.
  • The Phoenix Phenomenon: Documented patterns of banned operators rebranding (e.g., Suprobhat to Victor Classic) to evade enforcement.
  • Economic Incentives: The "Joma" system creates a rational incentive for racing, directly leading to pedestrian fatalities.

This investigation serves as a citable primary record, consolidating over 155 sources to map accident hotspots and decode the political economy of Dhaka's transport sector.
